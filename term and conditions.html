Terms and Conditions for Visiting Monuments

1. Admission

By purchasing a ticket and entering the monument premises, you agree to abide by all rules and regulations set forth by the management.

2. Conduct

Visitors are expected to behave respectfully and responsibly. Any disruptive or inappropriate behavior may result in expulsion from the premises.

3. Photography

Photography is allowed for personal use only. Commercial photography or any use for monetary gain is strictly prohibited without prior permission.

4. Preservation of Heritage

Help us preserve the monument's heritage by not defacing or causing damage to any structure or artifact. Please follow all posted guidelines.

5. Safety

For your safety and the safety of others, please follow all safety guidelines and instructions provided by monument staff. Emergency exits and evacuation procedures should be noted.

6. Tickets

All ticket sales are final. No refunds or exchanges will be issued unless the monument is closed due to unforeseen circumstances.

7. Children and Minors

Children must be accompanied by an adult. Parents or guardians are responsible for the behavior and safety of their children during the visit.

8. Accessibility

We strive to provide accessible facilities for all visitors. If you have specific accessibility requirements, please contact us in advance.

9. Changes to Terms

These terms and conditions are subject to change without notice. It is the responsibility of the visitor to stay informed about any updates.
